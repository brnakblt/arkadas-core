# Product Definition - Arkadaş Özel Eğitim ERP

Arkadaş Özel Eğitim is a modern, AI-integrated ERP system designed for the management of special education centers.

## Key Stakeholders
- **Admins:** Manage center operations, staff, and finances.
- **Teachers:** Track student progress and generate BEP reports.
- **Parents:** Access student progress and center communication.
- **Students:** The primary focus of the education and support.

## Core Value Proposition
1. **AI Integration:** Automates tedious tasks like BEP (Bireyselleştirilmiş Eğitim Programı) generation.
2. **Unified Platform:** Integrates PBX (phone), SFTP (files), and Mebbis (government system).
3. **Data Security:** Strict "Fail-Closed" authentication to protect sensitive student data.
