# Product Definition - Arkadaş Özel Eğitim ERP

## Product Vision
The Arkadaş Özel Eğitim ERP is a modern, comprehensive management platform designed to digitize and optimize the operations of Special Education and Rehabilitation Centers. By integrating advanced technologies like AI and biometrics, the system aims to reduce administrative burden, ensure data security, and enhance the quality of education and communication for all stakeholders.

## Target Users
- **Administrative Staff:** Manage billing, personnel, enrollment, and institutional reporting.
- **Educators & Teachers:** Track student progress, generate Individualized Education Programs (BEP) using AI assistance, and manage classroom attendance.
- **Parents & Guardians:** Access student progress reports, track service routes, and communicate with the institution.
- **Drivers & Field Staff:** Utilize real-time service route tracking and manage student pickup/drop-off sequences.

## Key Modules & Features
- **Student & Personnel Management:** Complete lifecycle management for students (enrollment to graduation) and personnel (HR, performance, and scheduling).
- **AI-Powered Education Planning:** Automated generation of BEP documents using Google Gemini, progress tracking, and educational material generation.
- **Biometric Operations:** Secure attendance tracking using facial recognition (BSDK simulation) and integrated service route management.
- **Unified Communication:** A centralized hub for notifications (SMS/WhatsApp), parent portals, and an AI-powered chatbot assistant for instant support.
- **Document Management:** Integrated document editing and secure file storage (SFTPGo/OnlyOffice) for institutional records.

## Goals & Success Criteria
- **Operational Excellence:** Achieve a significant reduction in manual paperwork and administrative processing time.
- **Reliable Attendance:** Implement a secure, biometric-based attendance system to ensure accurate tracking and government compliance.
- **Enhanced Transparency:** Foster trust through real-time communication and transparent progress reporting for parents and guardians.
- **Data-Driven Insights:** Provide institutional management with actionable metrics on performance, finance, and educational outcomes.

## Critical Integrations
- **Government Systems:** Bi-directional synchronization with MEBBIS for regulatory compliance.
- **Communication Channels:** Seamless integration with SMS and WhatsApp Business APIs for automated alerting.
- **Security & Infrastructure:** Robust secret management via Infisical and scalable document services through SFTPGo and OnlyOffice.
