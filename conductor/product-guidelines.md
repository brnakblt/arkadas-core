# Product Guidelines - Arkadaş Özel Eğitim ERP

## Tone & Voice
The Arkadaş Özel Eğitim ERP system communicates with a **Friendly, Encouraging, and Accessible** tone. While the data being handled is sensitive and institutional, the interaction should feel supportive of the educational mission. Language should be clear and welcoming to all stakeholders, from administrators to parents.

## Visual Identity & Design Principles
- **Brand Consistency:** The system strictly adheres to the institutional visual identity. This includes consistent use of the Arkadaş logo, official brand colors, and specified typography across all modules (Strapi, Next.js, and documentation).
- **Professional Polish:** Every screen should reflect the institution's commitment to quality. Layouts should be balanced, with a clear hierarchy of information.
- **Visual Feedback:** Provide immediate and clear feedback for user actions (e.g., success toasts, loading states) to maintain user confidence.

## Content & Documentation Standards
- **Step-by-Step Tutorials:** Technical and user documentation must prioritize visual, sequential guides. Administrative tasks should be broken down into clear, manageable steps with accompanying screenshots or diagrams where possible.
- **Accessibility in Language:** Help content should be written to be understood by everyone. Complex processes should be simplified without losing necessary detail.
- **Structured Navigation:** Information within the app and documentation should be easily searchable and well-indexed to minimize time spent searching for help.

## Functional Priorities
- **User Empowerment:** Design features that allow users to complete tasks independently and efficiently.
- **Safety First:** Security and data privacy principles are non-negotiable and should be communicated clearly to users when they interact with sensitive PII or biometric data.
